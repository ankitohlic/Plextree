How It Works

Our Journey of planting trees in 5 simple steps.

STEP 1

We will receive money through your donations.

STEP 2

Your sapling will then be planted on ground.

STEP 3

We will use your donated money for not only buying the sapling but also for its maintenance, fertilizer, water, and the plant's security.

STEP 4

At the end of every month, you will recieve an image of your own plant with the name you have given to it on a board next to it. You can even share it with your friends and family.

STEP 5

You will recieve your tree plantation certificate.

STEP 6

After 3 years since you donated, your pledge to take care of the plant will be over (so no more donation will be required), and the plant will be strong enough to survive further.
